Our Story

What was initially known as LEAFO Productions, initially founded as a means to monetize our passions and creativity, has evolved into Ev0la (Expressive Visions of limitless art), a transformative creative agency dedicated to providing a platform for young African creatives to freely express themselves and pursue their creative passions without compromising their career choices for financial stability.
