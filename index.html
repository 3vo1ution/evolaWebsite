As the founder of Evola, Tlhalefo Boshielo, I intimately understand the challenges faced by young African artists, especially in the context of in South Africa. We have often been discouraged from pursuing our passions and told that only careers in Science, Medicine, or Engineering can lead to a fulfilling life. This narrative resonates with countless African artists who have had to suppress their aspirations and conform to paths not of their own choosing, a situation I myself narrowly escaped.
At Evola, our primary objective is to address this issue head-on, giving artists a platform to thrive and and excell.
This site marks a step in that journey, a platform that serves as a display of African excellence
